⚠ Emerging-threat watch list interpretive
A curated set of the current major outbreak / health-emergency threats tracked by WHO Disease Outbreak News, CDC and Africa CDC. This is qualitative early-warning context — not a live count — and reflects status as of the embed date below.
Compiled from public outbreak bulletins: WHO Disease Outbreak News (DON), CDC travel/health notices, Africa CDC, and PAHO. There is no single CORS-open live API for these, so entries are curated and dated, not streamed. Status labels (e.g. "active", "expanding", "watch") are editorial summaries of the cited authorities, not formal risk ratings. Reporting lags are large and absence of data is not absence of disease — surveillance is weakest exactly where outbreaks are often largest.

Data & method. Mpox: Our World in Data mpox dataset (CSV, compiled from WHO & national surveillance), pulled live in-browser when reachable, else from an embedded snapshot whose vintage is shown above; cumulative confirmed cases/deaths. Measles: CDC measles surveillance annual confirmed-case totals (cdc.gov/measles) — embedded MEASURED snapshot (the per-year rollup has no clean CORS-open live API; the weekly NNDSS tables are split by year and lag). COVID-19: disease.sh aggregator (/v3/covid-19/all and /countries), live when reachable; cumulative pandemic totals, not current incidence. Watch list: curated from WHO DON, CDC, Africa CDC & PAHO bulletins.
Honesty. Cumulative counts grow monotonically and revise as reports arrive; recent periods under-count. Reporting is uneven across countries and absence of data is not absence of disease. Mpox per-country "latest" dates differ, so cumulative totals are not all current to the same day. COVID figures are largely frozen historical totals. The watch list and country comparisons are interpretive context, not live risk ratings. This tool is descriptive, not predictive; it does not forecast, advise, or rank threats. No personal or protected health information is used.
