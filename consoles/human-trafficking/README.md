# Human Trafficking — Awareness, Data & Get Help

A single self-contained `index.html` public-information resource. It has three jobs: **connect people to help**,
**explain what the public evidence does and doesn't say**, and **teach the recognized warning signs** — without
ever profiling or scoring an individual.

- **No dependencies, no keys, no tracking.** Pure HTML/CSS/JS. It works offline and from `file://`, so the
  hotline numbers always render even with no internet.
- **Every number and phone number was verified against its primary source** before it went in (a research pass
  plus an independent adversarial fact-check). Figures are cited point-in-time snapshots, not live feeds — follow
  the source links for the latest.

## Open it
Double-click `index.html`, or host the folder anywhere static (GitHub Pages, Netlify, Cloudflare Pages) and share the URL.

## What's inside
1. **Get help & report safely** — verified hotlines + a *safe-response* protocol (report, don't confront) + an
   interactive "pick your situation" router.
2. **The scale** — 2022 Global Estimates of Modern Slavery (ILO/Walk Free/IOM), with an explicit
   *estimate ≠ detected ≠ reported* explainer.
3. **US & Florida** — National Human Trafficking Hotline 2024 statistics (with Polaris's own "this is not
   prevalence" caveat) and a Florida focus.
4. **Global** — Walk Free prevalence vs absolute, UNODC detected-victim patterns, the State Dept TIP tier system,
   G20 supply-chain exposure, and the IOM CTDC case dataset.
5. **Spot the signs** — ILO / DHS / Polaris indicator frameworks, each wrapped in the mandatory
   "indicators aren't proof; race/sex/religion are never indicators; report, don't confront."

## Key hotlines (verified June 2026)
- **National Human Trafficking Hotline:** 1-888-373-7888 · text 233733 · humantraffickinghotline.org — 24/7, confidential, 200+ languages, **not** law enforcement.
- **Emergency / immediate danger:** 911
- **ICE HSI Tip Line (DHS):** 1-866-347-2423 (intl 802-872-6199) · ice.gov/tipline
- **NCMEC CyberTipline (online child exploitation):** 1-800-843-5678 · report.cybertip.org
- **Florida Abuse Hotline:** 1-800-962-2873 · **FDLE statewide tip line:** 1-855-352-7233

> Hotline numbers are safety-critical. If you reuse this page, re-verify them periodically against the official sites.

## Scope & honesty
This is an independent, non-commercial resource — not affiliated with or endorsed by any agency or cited
organization, and not legal/professional advice. It deliberately omits widely-circulated trafficking statistics
that could not be confirmed in a primary source. It does **not** profile, score, or surveil individuals; it exists
to connect people to help.
