The acute-harm signal — long waits MEASURED

Patients waiting over 8 and over 12 hours in major EDs in the latest week. Long ED waits are independently associated with increased 30-day mortality.

Bed occupancy by health board MEASURED · 2025 Q4

Average occupancy of staffed acute beds. Above ~85% (amber) flow slows; above ~92% (red) safety and infection risk rise sharply.

Delayed discharges — flow blockers MEASURED

Patients clinically ready to leave but unable to (the "no criteria to reside" backlog) at the monthly census — the upstream cause of full beds and A&E gridlock. By board.
